Section 01

Overview & Who We Are

Welcome to HomeReno Picks ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate a curated product guide website focused on helping home builders, renovation contractors, and families across the United States find high-quality, energy-efficient home improvement and kitchen appliance products.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information when you visit our website hosted at GitHub Pages. We are committed to being transparent about our practices and respecting your privacy at every step.

🔑 Short version: We run a simple, informational website with affiliate links to Amazon. We do not collect personal data. We do not run ads, sell data, or create user accounts.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Because HomeReno Picks is a static informational website with no registration, login, or forms, we collect very limited information:

  • Automatically collected data: When you visit our site, standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is handled by GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not independently access or store these logs.
  • Affiliate click data: When you click a "View on Amazon" button, Amazon may set cookies on your browser to track purchases. This data is collected and controlled by Amazon, not by us. See Section 5 for full details.

ℹ️ We do not use contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, or any data entry fields on our website. We do not collect any personal information from visitors.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use any information we receive solely for the following purposes:

  • To understand general traffic patterns on our site so we can improve the content we offer (using anonymized, aggregated data only).
  • To maintain and operate our website in accordance with GitHub Pages hosting requirements.
  • To fulfill our obligations as an Amazon Associate, including ensuring our affiliate links are correctly formatted and our disclosures are visible.

We do not use your information to send you unsolicited emails, serve you advertisements, build behavioral profiles, or sell to third-party data brokers.

Section 04

Cookies & Tracking

Our own website does not place any first-party cookies or tracking scripts on your device. However, when you click an affiliate link to Amazon, Amazon's website will use its own cookies and tracking mechanisms to identify purchases that originated from our referral links.

Amazon's affiliate tracking cookie typically lasts 24 hours. If you purchase a product within 24 hours of clicking our link, we may receive a small commission from Amazon at no additional cost to you.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not prevent you from visiting our site or viewing our content, though it may affect how Amazon's cart and checkout functions work.

ℹ️ For more information on how to control cookies, visit AllAboutCookies.org, a free consumer resource.

🛒

Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases.

HomeReno Picks participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

This means that when you click a "View on Amazon" button on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission. The price you pay is exactly the same — our commission comes from Amazon's existing margin, not from any markup charged to you.

Our product recommendations are based entirely on research, energy efficiency ratings, suitability for typical US home sizes, and long-term value for homeowners. We only feature products we genuinely believe are a strong fit for our audience. Affiliate commissions do not influence which products we feature or how we describe them.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

HomeReno Picks is intended for adults, including homeowners, contractors, and families managing home renovation projects. Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our site, please be aware that our site has no forms or data entry fields, and we do not collect such information.

Section 08

Data Security

Since our website is a static HTML site with no database, forms, or user account system, we hold no personal data about our visitors.

Our site is served over HTTPS via GitHub Pages, which means all data transmitted between your browser and our site is encrypted in transit. We take reasonable precautions to protect any personal information we do receive, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Section 09

Your Rights & Choices

As a visitor to our site, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Opt-Out of Amazon Tracking: You can opt out of Amazon's interest-based advertising at amazon.com/adprefs.
  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • Do Not Track: Our static website does not run tracking scripts, so no additional Do Not Track (DNT) configuration is needed on our end.

ℹ️ California residents: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not collect or sell personal data.

ℹ️ Virginia residents: Under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), you have rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of targeted advertising. We do not conduct targeted advertising or collect personal data.
